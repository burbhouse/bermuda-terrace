Association

The Bermuda Terrace Property Owners Association (BTPOA) is a voluntary organization. The annual association fee is $50.00 per household.

The BTPOA manages the Front Entrance at Tequesta Drive, the Common Area, Dry Slips, and Wet Slips (Dock).

The Common area is located at 19482 Gulfstream Way. Some of the amenities include the BTPOA's private boat ramp, the Tiki Hut, fresh water, asphalt paving and parking and a private white sand beach. Vehicular access to the Common Area and its amenities are available for a fee of $55.00 billed annually. There is also a one time FOB/Clicker Fee. To obtain a FOB/Clicker please contact the Dockmaster.
